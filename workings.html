Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about using FindHouse.

How much does it cost to list my property?

Listing your property on FindHouse is completely free. We only take a small percentage when a successful handover is completed.

How do I get my 10% reward?

Once the new tenant confirms the handover, your 10% reward is automatically processed and transferred to your account within 7 business days.

Is FindHouse available nationwide?

Yes, FindHouse is available across all states in Nigeria.

Can landlords list properties too?

Currently, FindHouse is focused on tenant-to-tenant handovers, but we're working on expanding our services to include landlords in the future.
