Our Mission

We're on a mission to revolutionize the rental market in Nigeria by connecting people directly, eliminating unnecessary fees, and creating a more efficient housing ecosystem.

Our Story

FindHouse was born out of frustration with the traditional rental market in Nigeria.

Our founder, a former executive at PropertyPro, witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by Nigerians looking for affordable housing options.

The traditional system, dominated by agents charging exorbitant fees, was making it increasingly difficult for people to find suitable accommodation within their budget.

That's when the idea struck: what if people leaving their apartments could connect directly with those looking for new homes?

Since our launch in 2022, we've helped thousands of Nigerians find homes easily and affordably, while rewarding those who list their properties.

Our Vision
